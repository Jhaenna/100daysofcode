Nail Trends for Spring 2022

Jheanna is back by popular demand to share Spring 2022 nail trends you'll want to show your nail tech.



Although it doesn't feel like it, spring is only 17 days away (March 20th) and we are ready for it. So, what better way to celebrate its arrival than by flaunting your spring nails? These fashion trends scream, "Winter, get out of here!"

Tasteful Nudes

Nothing is more elegant than nude-toned nails, and many colorful nail trends have taken a back seat as a result of this color palette. Still not convinced? Simply scrolling through this article will demonstrate the power of nakedness.

Animal Print

With this animal print trend, you can embrace your wild side (or spirit animal). They'll be able to spot you in any room with these nails.

Rhinestones + Gems

This trend is the way to go if you want to be classy and bougie! Simply request that your nail technician add some studs, diamonds, or pearls to your manicure, and you'll be able to channel your inner material girl.

Florals

Florals for spring...innovative! You can never go wrong with a floral accent in your spring manicure. The possibilities are endless with different types of florals and colors!

Pink Gradient

Did someone say fifty different shades of pink? This trend has a special place in our hearts because it is where we both began our funky nail journey. And we are overjoyed that it is back.

Hearts After Valentines Day

Hearts after Valentine's Day are like white after Labor Day in that they are always a good idea! Don't limit your nail designs to the holidays; hearts and pastel pinks are always in style.
